Creative Process

01

Consultation

This is where the creative fun begins. We work directly with you, listening to your wants and needs, in order to provide a strategic production plan that will help you accomplish your goals.

02

Pre-Production

This is essentially a game plan for our production. We conduct location scouts, identify shooting locations, develop a schedule, and coordinate logistics with client, crew, and talent.

03

Production

This is where the creative concept comes to life, from staging and framing to lighting, sound, and video. Our team operates with a keen attention to detail and collective goal of serving you well.

04

Post-Production

In this final phase, we build the story arc, make cuts, insert b-roll, add SFX, license msuic, color grade footage, create graphics, and share drafts with you for feedback.
